# Authorized data use

The anonymized datasets in `data/` are provided for authorized scholarly use
only, including manuscript review, academic verification, and non-commercial
research reproduction of the associated NBGA study.

Use of the datasets requires attribution to the associated manuscript and this
repository. Users are not permitted to attempt re-identification, reconstruct
source asset identifiers, redistribute the datasets as a standalone data
product, or use the datasets for commercial purposes without written permission
from the corresponding authors.

For uses beyond academic review and non-commercial research reproduction,
contact the corresponding authors to request permission.
